Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 30, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how VitaMeal collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our meal planning service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account with VitaMeal, we collect:

Data Type Examples Purpose
Account Information Username, email address, password Account creation and authentication
Personal Details Age, gender, weight, height Personalized meal planning and calorie calculations
Health & Fitness Data Activity level, fitness goals, dietary restrictions Customized nutrition recommendations
Meal Preferences Preferred/blocked ingredients and dishes Tailored meal plan generation
Meal Plans Weekly meal schedules, recipe selections Service delivery and meal tracking

1.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device type and operating system
  • Usage patterns and app interactions
  • Log files and error reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide personalized meal plans based on your dietary goals and preferences
  • Calculate nutritional needs using your physical characteristics and activity level
  • Generate recipe recommendations while avoiding blocked ingredients
  • Maintain your account and provide customer support
  • Improve our service through usage analytics and user feedback
  • Send important notifications about your account or service updates

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Password Protection

All user passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing with a minimum of 10 salt rounds, ensuring your login credentials remain secure.

3.2 Data Storage

Your information is stored in secure databases with:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Backup and recovery procedures

3.3 User Roles and Access

Access to your data is limited based on user roles:

  • Users: Can only access their own personal data
  • Cooks: Can access recipe and dish information, but not personal user data
  • Admins: Have system access for support and maintenance purposes only

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly agree to information sharing
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Service providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our service (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Safety and security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of VitaMeal or our users

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of all personal information we have about you
  • Update your information: Modify your profile, preferences, and meal plans at any time
  • Delete your account: Request complete removal of your account and associated data
  • Data portability: Export your meal plans and preference data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications

6. Cookies and Tracking

VitaMeal uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login status and preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our service
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • Necessary to provide our services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your privacy rights according to this policy.

9. Children's Privacy

VitaMeal is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • Prominent notice within the VitaMeal application
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

11. Third-Party Services

VitaMeal may integrate with third-party services (such as Google Sign-In). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.
